Jay String Quartet

"To hear the Jay String Quartet is to hear chamber music afresh and be transported back to an earlier age ..." The Financial Times

“Gorgeous – the Jays evoke not just the visual world of the early twentieth century, but the breezes and summer blooms”Gramophone

Our new recording, Painting with Music, takes the two classic “impressionist” quartets of Debussy and Ravel, and sets them against a recording of the new work for string quartet by composer and painter Charlie Seaborn, “Fractured colour”, written for the Jay String Quartet.
