Jay String Quartet

"To hear the Jay String Quartet is to hear chamber music afresh and be transported back to an earlier age ..." The Financial Times

Painting with music

“Gorgeous – the Jays evoke not just the visual world of the early twentieth century, but the breezes and summer blooms”Gramophone

Our new recording, Painting with Music, takes the two classic “impressionist” quartets of Debussy and Ravel, and sets them against a recording of the new work for string quartet by composer and painter Charlie Seaborn. “Fractured colour” was written especially for the Jay String Quartet, at a time when our visual world has never been more varied or fractured.

“There's a fluidity to the Jay’s playing of both works that suits the music's character, a mood of wistfulness too that the Ravel especially benefits from.”The Sunday Times

“I wondered whether there was anything new to be said about these two incessantly recorded and frequently paired works. Happily the answer is 'yes'.”Presto Classical
