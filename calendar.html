Beakfield Festival of Music & Painting

We're thrilled to be able to say that we've been able to bring musicians and painters together again at last at Beakfield for the fourth Beakfield Festival of Music and Painting.

Every year with friends and colleagues in the arts we put together a programme of music that is connected in some way to the visual arts.

This year the theme will be "music and colour".
