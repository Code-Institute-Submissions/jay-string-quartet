Beakfield Festival of Music & Painting

Music and colour

We're thrilled to say that we've been able to bring musicians and painters together again at last at Beakfield for the fourth Beakfield Festival of Music and Painting.

Every year with friends and colleagues in the arts we put together a programme of music that is connected in some way to the visual arts.

This year the theme will be "music and colour".

Find out more

The painting is Unravelling Bolero by Anne Adams, a bar-by-bar representation of the popular classical piece Boléro by Maurice Ravel.
