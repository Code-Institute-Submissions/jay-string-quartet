Jay String Quartet

The Jay String Quartet is recognised as an important new voice in chamber music. They came together as a quartet in London in 2016, and quickly formed a bond over a shared enthusiasm for the music of the late nineteenth and early twentieth centuries. With success at the Montreal prix, they have performed at major venues in North America, Europe, and Japan.
