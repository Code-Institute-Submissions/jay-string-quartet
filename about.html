About the Jay String Quartet

"A new voice in chamber music ..." BBC Radio 3

The Jay String Quartet is recognised as making an important new contribution to chamber music. They came together as a quartet in London in 2016, and quickly formed a bond over a shared enthusiasm for the music of the late nineteenth and early twentieth centuries. Following success at the Montreal prix, they have performed at major venues in North America, Europe, and Japan.

We also work with composers and musicians in other genres, and are proud to have premiered a number of chamber pieces. We are devoted to all the arts and work with visual artists, choreographers, and writers in developing new work.

The quartet is enthusiastically devoted to music in the community in the widest sense. We are keen supporters of the Strings for Schools initiative, and work closely with primary and secondary pupils. We are passionate about developing new voices, talents, and insights from different communities.
